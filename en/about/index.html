About me

I’m Félix Luginbühl, a data analyst, R developer and Shiny app architect. I build data tools for organizations.

Side projects

I developed three R packages:

  • BFS to download data from the Swiss Federal Statistical Office.
  • wikisourcer to download public domain works from Wikisource.
  • polyglot to learn foreign language vocabulary in the R console.
To reproduce the blog posts, you can use:

  • My repository on Github.
  • My RStudio server on Binder.
